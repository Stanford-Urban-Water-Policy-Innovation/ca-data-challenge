Coping with the California Drought: 2014-2016

These are interactive visualizations of average water use per capita for all reporting utilities in California from January 2013 to September 2016. Water use in 2013 was used as a baseline to compute water savings in 2014-2016. State-mandated conservation goals were set based on residential water use per capita in 2013 and tailored to individual utilities baselines.

*Only data from June 2014 to September 2016 was available at the time these graphs were generated.
